Saffron Rose Water Bastani

Last time I had authentic Saffron Rose Water Bastani was at the Moghadam Museum, a Historic Priceless House in Tehran.

Kebab Koobideh

Kuku Sabzi

Kuku, which is like a Persian frittata, comes in many forms, but this one, packed to the brim with herbs, is my favorite.

Kuku Bademjan

Kuku Bademjan is an egg-based dish much like a frittata, having said that this rendition is with fluffy cooked eggplants.

Burger

Although hamburgers are American, burgers are thriving at Burger House, a Fast Food Restaurant in Tehran.

Shirini Danmarki

Shirini Danmarki is a Persian pastry, similar to a Danish, as in a multilayered, creme-filled decadent sweet.
